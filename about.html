About Me

Welcome, Stranger

Hey there stranger, thanks for taking the time to pop in to my corner of the increasingly impersonal internet. For the purpose of privacy, you can call me Diomedes. I am an independent writer/editor looking to break into the media landscape.

I've had the wonderful pleasure of, and crippling curse, of studying multiple disciplines, from rigorous academic writing to game development. Long story short, I have done a lot. Turns out, most endeavors require the same fundamental elements: compelling narratives, well-researched foundations, and a deep understanding of human nature.

In the realm of my media pursuits, I intend to approach each and every project with as much curiosity and imagination as I can manage at any given moment. If you are anything like me, you'll know that this can vary wildly depending on the day. My ultimate goal is to reach a place where I create impactful content, whether that is via the traditional written word or in a more interactive format remains to be seen.
