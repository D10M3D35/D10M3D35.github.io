About Me

Welcome, Stranger

Hey there stranger, thanks for taking the time to pop in to my corner of the increasingly impersonal internet. I go by Diomedes, and I am an independent writer/editor looking to break into the media landscape.

My work spans multiple disciplines, from rigorous academic research to immersive fictional worlds. I believe that great storytelling, whether in scholarly papers or fantasy epics, requires the same fundamental elements: compelling narratives, well-researched foundations, and a deep understanding of human nature.

Drawing inspiration from classical literature and mythology, I approach each project with the curiosity of a scholar and the imagination of a storyteller. My goal is to create content that not only informs or entertains but leaves a lasting impact on readers.

My Journey

Like my namesake from Homer's Iliad, I believe in the power of both wisdom and courage in pursuing one's craft. My writing journey has taken me through the halls of academia, the realms of fantasy, and now into the interactive worlds of game development.

Each medium presents its own challenges and rewards, but they all share a common thread: the desire to connect with readers and players on a meaningful level.
