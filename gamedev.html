Vision & Goals

My goal in game writing is to elevate the medium's storytelling potential. I believe games can be powerful vehicles for exploring complex themes and emotions, much like literature or film.

By combining my academic research skills, fiction writing experience, and passion for interactive media, I aim to create gaming experiences that are both entertaining and meaningful.
